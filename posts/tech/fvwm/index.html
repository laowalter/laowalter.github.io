mouse cursor

%filename: ~/.Xresources

 18 Xcursor.theme: ComixCursors-White
 19 Xcursor.size: 64

Fvwm console font setup

%filename: ~/.Xresources

 40 FvwmConsole*VT100*geometry: 40x4
 41 FvwmConsole*font: xft:Inconsolata LGC:pixelsize=16

Windows Title Bar 等各种中文字体支持

安装字体

$ emerge -avq media-fonts/wqy-zenhei
$ fc-list|grep WenQuanYi
/usr/share/fonts/wqy-zenhei/wqy-zenhei.ttc: WenQuanYi Zen Hei:style=Regular
/usr/share/fonts/wqy-zenhei/wqy-zenhei.ttc: WenQuanYi Zen Hei Sharp,文泉驛點陣正黑:style=Regular
/usr/share/fonts/wqy-zenhei/wqy-zenhei.ttc: WenQuanYi Zen Hei Mono:style=Regular

配置fvwm的配置文件

将原来的缺省配置：
    Font "xft:Sans:Bold:size=8:antialias=True"
全部改为：
    Font "xft:WenQuanYi Zen Hei:Sans:Bold:size=8:antialias=True"
